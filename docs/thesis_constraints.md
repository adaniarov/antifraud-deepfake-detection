# docs/thesis_constraints.md

## Writing style
- Main explanatory language: Russian
- Code, prompts, and configuration examples: English
- Tone: formal, academic, conservative

## What not to do
- Do not invent metrics.
- Do not fabricate datasets, sample sizes, or experiment outcomes.
- Do not overclaim real-world generalization.
- Do not ignore dataset limitations.

## Required discussion points when relevant
- temporal bias
- genre imbalance
- held-out generator evaluation
- partition-based interpretation
- robustness limitations
- practical anti-fraud relevance

## Thesis structure anchor
1. Introduction
2. Related work
3. Dataset and task setup
4. Methodology
5. Experiments and results
6. Prototype / implementation
7. Conclusion
